About

Join us every Wednesday at Namaste for our Chess Night, a free-to-play event where players of all ages and skill levels are welcome!

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, it's a fun and relaxed environment to test your skills and any new strategies!

Whilst at Namaste you can enjoy a pint and a delicious serving of pakora while you play for just £10.

No need to be an expert – just come along, have fun, and maybe even learn a few new tricks!

When

We meet every Wednesday from 6 pm at Namaste by Delhi Darbar located conveniently in the St Enoch Centre.

Namaste by Delhi Darbar
1st Floor next to Vue Cinema
G1 4BW

Parking is available in the St Enochs Car Park for £2 all night from 5pm.
